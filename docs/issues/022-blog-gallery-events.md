## What to build

Content-driven public pages — blog, gallery, events, news, feedback.

1. **Health Blog** — Article listing with categories, detail page, search, recent posts sidebar
2. **Photo Gallery** — Albums with cover image, lightbox view, captions
3. **Video Gallery** — Embedded videos (YouTube/Vimeo), categories
4. **Events & News** — Upcoming events calendar, health camps, CME programs, press releases archive
5. **Feedback & Reviews** — Patient feedback form, rating (1-5 stars), admin approves before public display
6. **Testimonials** — Display approved testimonials on homepage (from CMS content blocks)

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Blog posts with categories editable from CMS
- [ ] Gallery albums with lightbox photo viewing
- [ ] Events shown on public page with calendar; past events archived
- [ ] Feedback form submits to admin for approval
- [ ] Approved testimonials appear on homepage
