## What to build

Patient self-service portal for accessing health information online.

1. **Patient Registration/Login** — Patient creates account using registered phone/email, OTP verification
2. **Dashboard** — Upcoming appointments, recent lab reports, outstanding bills
3. **Medical History** — Diagnoses, prescriptions, lab reports — read-only view
4. **View Lab Reports** — Download lab report PDFs
5. **View Prescriptions** — Past prescription history with details
6. **Bill View & Payment** — View bills, download invoice, pay online
7. **Appointment History** — Past and upcoming appointments
8. **Profile Management** — Update contact info, emergency contact, password change
9. **Feedback** — Submit rating and feedback for visits

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Patient can register and login with OTP
- [ ] Dashboard shows upcoming appointments and recent reports
- [ ] Lab reports downloadable as PDF
- [ ] Bills visible with payment status
- [ ] Profile updates reflected in admin system

## Blocked by

#4 (Patient Management), #9 (Lab Management), #11 (Billing)
