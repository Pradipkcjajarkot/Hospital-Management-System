## What to build

Radiology & Imaging management — orders, modality scheduling, and reporting.

1. **Imaging Test Catalog** — X-ray, CT, MRI, Ultrasound, Mammography — with protocols and preparation
2. **Imaging Order** — Doctor orders imaging for patient with clinical history and reason
3. **Modality/Room Scheduling** — Assign imaging room and time slot
4. **Image Upload** — Upload DICOM/images (basic viewer), link to patient order
5. **Radiologist Reporting** — Radiologist views images and enters report (findings, impression)
6. **Report Approval** — Report reviewed and finalized by senior radiologist
7. **Contrast Tracking** — Record contrast used, patient allergy history
8. **Patient Imaging History** — Timeline of all imaging studies with reports

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Imaging order can be placed from OPD/IPD consultation
- [ ] Modality schedule shows booked slots
- [ ] Images uploaded and associated with patient order
- [ ] Radiologist report created and approved before visible to doctor
- [ ] Patient imaging history shows all past studies
