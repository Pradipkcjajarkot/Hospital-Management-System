## What to build

Inventory & store management for medical supplies and equipment.

1. **Item Catalog** — Medical supplies, consumables, equipment with categories, units, min/max stock levels
2. **Stock Management** — Goods Receipt Note (GRN), stock issue, stock transfer between departments
3. **Requisition Management** — Department raises requisition → approval → issue from store
4. **Purchase Order Workflow** — Create PO → Approval → Send to supplier → Receive → GRN
5. **Supplier Management** — Supplier master, contact, payment terms, rating
6. **Asset Management** — Equipment tracking (location, maintenance schedule, warranty, depreciation)
7. **Stock-Taking** — Periodic stock count, variance reporting
8. **Reports** — Stock status, consumption patterns, reorder level alerts

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Items categorized and searchable with current stock quantity
- [ ] GRN updates stock; issue reduces stock
- [ ] Requisition → approval workflow works end-to-end
- [ ] Low stock items highlighted on dashboard
- [ ] Stock-taking can be done and variance adjusted
