## What to build

Doctor management module for profiles, schedules, and assignments.

1. **Doctor Profiles** — Name, qualifications, specialization, experience, registration number, photo, biography
2. **Department Assignment** — Assign doctor to one or more departments
3. **Schedule Management** — Weekly recurring schedules with time slots, breaks, buffer time
4. **Leave Management** — Doctor leave with dates, reason; blocks appointment slots
5. **Consultation Fees** — Different fees for OPD, IPD, online consultation
6. **Doctor Dashboard** — View today's appointments, pending prescriptions
7. **Performance Metrics** — Patients seen, surgeries, revenue generated

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Admin can create/edit doctor profiles with all details
- [ ] Doctor assigned to department appears under that department
- [ ] Weekly schedule with time slots visible on appointment booking
- [ ] Leave blocks appointment slots automatically
- [ ] Doctor can log in and see their dashboard
